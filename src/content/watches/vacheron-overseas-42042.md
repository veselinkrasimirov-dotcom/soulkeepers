The hearthstone of the lineage. Born late into a category Patek and Audemars Piguet had already defined, untroubled by it.

The first-generation Overseas is 37mm — a size the market has since decided is too small, which means you can currently buy one of the best-finished steel sport watches ever made at a price that reflects the market's mistake rather than the object's quality.

Mirror-polished on surfaces you will never see unless you remove the bracelet. That gap — the polish that exists for no one — is the whole case for Vacheron.

Maison-serviced. Service record enclosed. Comes with original box.
